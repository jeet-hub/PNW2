Making the Paradigm Shift – About Power X 2.0

Tech-Enabled Power. Scalable Digi Infra.
One Platform.


As Nigeria catapults towards a tech-first, digital economy, the power sector stands at a critical inflection point. Reliable, intelligent, and integrated energy systems are no longer optional, they are foundational. Power X 2.0 emerges at this pivotal moment, evolving from Power & Water Nigeria into a future-focused platform where energy meets technology, and where digital infrastructure development aligns with national ambition. Bringing together stakeholders across generation, transmission, distribution, energy storage, and renewables, Power X 2.0 fosters the convergence of utilities, digital infrastructure and high-growth industries like data centers, smart cities, telecom, finance and manufacturing all reliant on resilient, scalable power. This is more than an event. It’s a catalyst for transformation, designed to activate investment, spark innovation and power the real engines of Nigeria’s economic growth.
